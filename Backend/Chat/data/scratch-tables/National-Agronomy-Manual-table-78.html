Spray nozzle selection, maintenance and opera- tion.10Reduces exposure-selecting appropriate nozzle and pressure for the application, with an emphasis on higher volume spray nozzles run at lower pressures, will produce larger droplets and a narrower droplet size distribution, which reduces spray drift. Proper nozzle spacing, boom height, and boom suspension, along with frequent calibration and replacement of worn nozzles and leaking tubing, can increase effi- cacy and reduce drift potential.
